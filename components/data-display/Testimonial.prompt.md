**Testimonial** — patient quote card with faded quote mark, gold stars, gradient initial avatar.

```jsx
<Testimonial
  rating={5}
  quote="Dr. Reyes explained my diabetes plan in plain language. I finally feel in control."
  author="Marlene P."
  detail="Type 2 diabetes · Sandusky"
/>
```

Keep quotes to 1–3 sentences. The avatar shows the author's first initial on a blue→teal gradient.
