**Alert** — inline status message with an icon, tinted background, colored border.

```jsx
<Alert variant="success" title="Appointment requested">We'll call you within one business day.</Alert>
<Alert variant="warning">Our Sandusky office closes early on Fridays.</Alert>
```

Variants: `info` · `success` · `warning` · `error`. Optional `title` for a bold lead line; pass `icon` to override the default glyph.
