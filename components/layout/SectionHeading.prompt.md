**SectionHeading** — the signature section intro used across the site: eyebrow → title → teal divider → subtitle.

```jsx
<SectionHeading
  align="center"
  eyebrow="Why Ohio Endocrinology"
  title="Endocrine care you can trust"
  subtitle="Board-certified specialists, same-week appointments, and a plan explained in plain language."
/>
```

`align="center"` centers everything and the divider. `divider` recolors the pill (`teal` default). `Divider` is also available standalone.
